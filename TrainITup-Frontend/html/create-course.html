Frequently Asked Question

Do I need any prior experience or knowledge of interior design to take this course?

No prior experience is required! This course is designed for beginners and covers all fundamental concepts from the ground up.

What topics will be covered in this course?

The course covers interior design basics, color theory, space planning, furniture selection, and practical design implementation.

How long does the course take, and is it self-paced?

Yes, it's completely self-paced! Most students complete the course in 4-6 weeks depending on their commitment.

Will I have access to any resources or support during the course?

Yes! You'll have access to downloadable resources, design templates, and our community forum for support.

Is there a refund policy for the course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the course content.
